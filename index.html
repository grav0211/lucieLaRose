Contribute

Now that my election campaign is well underway, you may contribute financially. All donations go towards publicity and communication. Contributions should be given to M. André de l’Étoile, Official Agent or to me.

Only the municipality’s registered voters can contribute.

  • The maximum authorized towards a candidate is 200$.
  • A total of 50$ or more is payable by cheque.
  • A tax receipt will be issued.

Tax Credit applicable:

  • To 85% on the first 50$;
  • To 75% on remaining, up to 200$;
  • To a maximum of 155$ per year.

Did You Know…

  • 1600s

    The region of Sainte-Anne-de-Bellevue was named Bout de L’Isle.

  • 1895

    The Village becomes the Town of Sainte-Anne-de-Bellevue.

  • 1913

    The first Union Church in Canada was built in Ste-Anne-de-Bellevue.

  • 1917

    James John Harpell, a community visionary, built the Garden City Press. He also developed a cooperative housing project Gardenvale for his workers.

  • 2003

    Ste-Anne parish celebrated its 300 years.
